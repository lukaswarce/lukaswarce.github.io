Researcher, Entrepreneur and Innovator

Christian Spana is a Canadian Researcher, Entrepreneur, and Innovator. Spana is a researcher at the University of Saskatchewan and a consultant and developer with extensive experience in technology environments, start-ups, and software companies. His ability to provide exceptional user experiences and integrate systems is based on creativity and resourcefulness. The areas in which he has expertise include blockchain, distributed systems, machine learning, deep learning, big data, full stack & web and mobile development, as well as others.

Currently, he owns three companies worldwide and invests in five technology companies.
